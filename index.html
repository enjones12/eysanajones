About Me


Welcome to my site! Here you will find interesting sewing and jewelry making projects to try out at home.

I am a self-taught seamstress and DIYer looking to share my talents with the world. My facination with sewing came from a beautiful African-print sundress my mother made in the second grade. I firmly believe the best way to learn a hobby is to be hands-on. Through trial and error I have made great strides in my craft and along the way I've picked up beading. Look at my projects for fun ideas, quick projects, and finished products.
